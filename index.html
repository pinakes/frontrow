Mission

We elevate the user experience at music concerts, sports, and other large format gatherings.

We build products that music and sport audiences can use to connect more deeply with the main stage. FrontRow highlights the details of what is happening live, and keeps the social aspect of the event.

What

The final measure of refinement for live concert audio.

Our patented technology allows FrontRow to broadcast live audio to the audience from the board mix thanks to a wireless system implemented at the venue that takes in consideration time correction between the physical and digital sound.

Problem

The ability to hear deeply and clearly into a musical performance diminishes with distance from the source because air, the atmosphere itself, is a poor medium for sound transmission. The farther one is from the source of music or the spoken word the less clear and intelligible the details and nuances become.

Solution

1

Precision audio, delivered via FrontRow enabled earbuds or headphones and hear the clean, Front of the House board mix.

2

You are not isolated, you can still receive and experience the sound wave from the loudspeakers but your ears are protected.

3

Your smartphone provides the location data needed to deliver the best synchronization between broadcast and surrounding audio.
